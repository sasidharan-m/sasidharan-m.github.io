About Me:

Hi! I'm Sasidharan Mahalingam. I'm a PhD student at the Portland State University. I am currently being advised by Dr. Atul Ingle. Before that I was a Computer Vision Engineer at Inferigence Quotient. I did my Master of Science at the University of California - Santa Cruz. I was part of the Computer Vision Lab at UCSC. I have worked in multiple software and hardware engineer positions at Intel, Nvidia, Garmin and Amazon. My interest in technology lies in the intersection of Computational Imaging and Computer Vision. I aspire to create cameras of the future that have applications in autonomous driving, photography and augumented and virtual reality.
